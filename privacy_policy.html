YCL Olympiad — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 21, 2026

Summary for Parents: YCL Olympiad is a children's quiz app for Olympiad preparation. We collect minimal data (child's first name, quiz scores) stored locally on your device. We do not sell, share, or use your child's data for advertising. You can delete all data at any time from the app's Settings menu.

1. About This App

YCL Olympiad ("the App") is an educational quiz application designed for children aged 6–15, helping them prepare for academic Olympiad competitions. The App is developed and operated by YCL ("we", "us", "our").

2. Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the Google Play Families Policy, and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect: photographs, videos, or audio recordings of children; precise geolocation; contact lists or social media accounts; advertising identifiers or cookies; email addresses or phone numbers of children.

5. How Data Is Used

All collected data is used solely to provide and improve the educational experience: personalizing quiz sessions based on the child's learning level (adaptive difficulty), scheduling review questions using spaced repetition (SM-2 algorithm), tracking daily practice streaks to encourage consistent learning, and improving question quality through aggregate (non-personal) analytics.

6. Data Storage and Security

All personal data (child's name, quiz scores, learning progress) is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database. We do not maintain user accounts or cloud profiles. Anonymous, aggregated performance data may be logged to a secure server for quality improvement purposes. This data cannot be used to identify any individual child.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your child's personal information with any third party. We do not use personal data for advertising or marketing purposes. We do not use any third-party analytics SDK that tracks individual children.

8. Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

Review data: View all stored learning data through the App's profile and session history screens.

Delete data: Delete all of your child's data at any time by opening Settings (parent-verified) and selecting "Delete All Data for [Child Name]". This permanently removes all quiz history, progress, and learning data from the device.

Withdraw consent: You may withdraw your consent at any time by deleting the App. All locally stored data is removed when the App is uninstalled.

Contact us: You may contact us at any time to request information about data we hold, using the contact details below.

9. Parental Consent

Before any data collection begins, the App requires parental verification (a multiplication problem that is difficult for young children but straightforward for adults). Upon first use, parents are presented with this Privacy Policy and must provide explicit consent by tapping "I Agree" before their child can use the App. No data is collected prior to this consent.

10. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Google Play Store: For app distribution. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Forms: For anonymous, aggregated quiz quality data only. No personal identifiers are transmitted.

We do not integrate any third-party advertising networks, social media SDKs, or behavioral analytics tools.

11. Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you choose to delete it or uninstall the App. We do not set automatic data expiration periods because retaining learning progress is valuable for the educational experience. You may delete data at any time through the Settings menu.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through an in-app notification. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

13. Compliance

This App complies with: the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) of the United States, the Google Play Families Policy, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India.
